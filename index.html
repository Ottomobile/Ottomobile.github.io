Ottawa Sights and Scenery

Hello world! Currently I am doing a work term here in Ottawa. Ottawa offers many beautiful sights and attractions.

Parliament Building

The main attraction in the nation's capital. On Canada Day, July 1st, many Canadians and tourists celebrate Canada's birthday by coming together to enjoy concerts and fireworks (see video below of Ottawa's Canada Day 2015 events

Sunday Bikedays

In the summer months, the city closes down the Sir John A. Macdonald Parkway road on Sunday mornings for bikers to ride. This stretch of road is about 10km and runs along the Ottawa River.
